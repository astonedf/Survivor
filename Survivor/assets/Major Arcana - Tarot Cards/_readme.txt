❖ About the Project and Process ❖

	Hello! Welcome to my ✦ Major Arcana ✦ tarot cards project!  
	These artworks were lovingly created and inspired by the mystical Marigold Tarot Cards.

	In the process of creating the central designs and details of each card, I drew inspiration from
	the card’s meaning, name, and its design in the Marigold, Marseilles, and Rider–Waite Tarot decks.

	As of May 2025——aka, when I'm writing this very README——I’m planning to expand the deck with the
	Minor Arcana sometime soon. But for now, feel free to dive in, explore it use it, and enjoy it! 

	Don't forget to check the ❖ License ❖ guidelines if you have any questions about usage and distribution!
